Electronic Air Cleaners

Electronic air cleaners are not filters. Instead, they create an electric field that ionizes and traps particles that pass through it.

Electrostatic Filters

Using static electricity, electrostatic air filters efficiently clean the air inside your home—permanently.

An electrostatic charge is generated by air flowing through a maze of static-prone fibers, attracting airborne particles like a magnet and removing them from the air you breathe. Better still, electrostatic air filters are washable air filters, which means that you don’t need to replace them.

Advantages to installing an electrostatic air filter:

  • No harmful ozone emissions
  • Reduces pollen, dust and airborne pollutants
  • Less house cleaning
  • Never needs replacing
  • High dust and particle holding capacity
