Maintenence Contracts

Probably the best thing that you can do for the efficiency and consistency of your HVAC system is to get it regularly inspected and tuned up by a professional. Zenit Service offers complete commercial and industrial HVAC maintenance contracts for all different types of systems.

Our maintenance program includes several visits each year during which our technicians will thoroughly inspect, clean and examine your entire HVAC system. They will clean out any components that are dirty and alert you if there are any necessary replacements or repairs that need to happen.
